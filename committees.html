Accreditation Steering Committee (ASC)

  • Mandate: Overall leadership, coordination, and oversight for the self-assessment process.
  • Chair: Principal/Head of School
  • Members: Deputy Head, Department Head Rep, Business Manager, KCWG Chairs, Data Lead

Data Management & Analysis Lead

  • Mandate: Oversees data collection plan, directory structure, and quality. Guides RATs, supports KCWGs.
  • Lead: Data Specialist/Assessment Coordinator

Required Analysis Teams (RATs)

  • Mandate: Analyze student performance, stakeholder feedback, and learning environment data. Report findings to KCWGs and ASC.
  • Student Performance RAT: Chair: Assessment Coordinator/Math Dept. Head
  • Stakeholder Feedback RAT: Chair: Counselor/Communications Lead
  • Learning Environment RAT: Chair: Instructional Coach/Assistant Principal

Culture of Learning KCWG

  • Mandate: Gather evidence, rate standards, draft narrative for Culture of Learning (Standards 1-6).
  • Chair: Counselor/Dean of Students
  • Members: Teachers, Support Staff, Parent Rep

Leadership for Learning KCWG

  • Mandate: Gather evidence, rate standards, draft narrative for Leadership for Learning (Standards 7-15).
  • Chair: Department Head/Assistant Principal
  • Members: Teachers, Board Member, Admin Staff

Engagement of Learning KCWG

  • Mandate: Gather evidence, rate standards, draft narrative for Engagement of Learning (Standards 16-23).
  • Chair: Curriculum Coordinator/Tech Integration Specialist
  • Members: Teachers, Librarian, Student Rep

Growth in Learning KCWG

  • Mandate: Gather evidence, rate standards, draft narrative for Growth in Learning (Standards 24-31).
  • Chair: Assessment Coordinator/Instructional Coach
  • Members: Teachers, Special Ed Lead, PD Coordinator

Executive Summary Team

  • Mandate: Drafts the Executive Summary based on input from all teams.
  • Lead: Accreditation Coordinator
  • Members: Principal/Head of School, Communications Lead/Writer

Quality Assurance & Review Committee (QARC)

  • Mandate: Independent review of the draft self-assessment report. Provide feedback to ASC and teams.
  • Chair: Senior Staff Member (not on ASC or KCWG Chair)
  • Members: Teachers (other depts/grades), Admin/Support Staff, External Peer Reviewer (optional)
