Accreditation Committees & Teams

The following committees and teams are involved in the Cognia accreditation process:

  • Accreditation Steering Committee (ASC)
  • Data Lead Team
  • RATS (Review and Technical Support)
  • KCWGs (Key Committee Working Groups)
  • Executive Summary Team
  • Quality Assurance Review Committee (QARC)

Each committee plays a vital role in ensuring a successful accreditation process.
