Accreditation Timeline

The Cognia accreditation process follows a structured timeline with key phases and milestones:

May – Aug 2025
Setup & Initial Data Gathering
  • Appoint teams and confirm roles
  • Kickoff meeting and orientation
  • Establish directory structure and permissions
  • Begin data collection (existing sources, surveys, observations)
  • Initial evidence review by KCWGs
Sep – Dec 2025
Data Analysis & Evidence Collection
  • Continue data collection and cleaning
  • Conduct observations and analyze data
  • KCWGs collect and organize evidence for each standard
  • Preliminary findings and progress check-ins
Jan – Mar 2026
Narrative Drafting & Initial Review
  • Draft narratives for required analyses and key characteristics
  • Finalize evidence collection and ratings
  • Compile first full draft of Self-Assessment Report
  • Submit draft to Quality Assurance & Review Committee (QARC)
Apr – May 2026
Revision & Finalization
  • QARC reviews and provides feedback
  • Teams revise sections as needed
  • Compile and approve final report
End of May 2026
Submission
  • Submit final Self-Assessment Report and evidence to Cognia
  • Celebrate milestone!
