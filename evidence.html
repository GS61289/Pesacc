Perception
Analyzed stakeholder survey results (respect, fairness, bias)
Documentation
Handbook sections on purpose/expectations; Evaluation reports of relevant programs (e.g., advisory); Site council minutes/membership lists

Perception
Analyzed student survey results (academic/non-academic support)
